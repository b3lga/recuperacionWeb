JCC

CREATIVE DIRECTOR AND ILLUSTRATOR

JCC is a music producer from Collado Villalba, Madrid. His studio is located in Villanueva del Pardillo, where he works independently as a beatmaker and music producer. he specializes in urban music and has collaborated with artist such as Grecas, Midas Alonso and Ozono Crew. In addition to music prodution, he handles recording, mixing, and project development within his own workspace.
