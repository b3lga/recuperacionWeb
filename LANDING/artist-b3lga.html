B3LGA

CREATIVE DIRECTOR AND ILLUSTRATOR

B3lga is a creative director from Madrid, focused on the urban music scene. He works independently creating visual identities for artists, with emphasis on album covers and promotional design. He has collaborated with Grecas, Child, and Dirty Suc, combining illustration and graphic design to give each project a distinct image. His work bridges visual storytelling and music, shaping how emerging artists present themselves.
