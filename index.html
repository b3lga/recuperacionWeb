WHO ARE WE

Umbra Lab is a collective of creatives who work behind the scenes. We’re designers, illustrators, photographers, producers, and visual artists pushing projects forward from the shadows – across music, culture, and beyond. For independent artists who want to be seen. For artists looking for a solid creative team. For people who just enjoy art and want to support emerging talent. We focus on the process, the people, and the real impact behind the work. No spotlight needed.

We organize events that bring the creative scene together.

Live music, artist markets, merch drops, and more – all designed to give visibility to emerging talent and connect people through art, music, and culture.

Spaces to discover, support, and vibe with real creatives doing real work.

HOW TO JOIN US

We’re always open to connecting with creatives who share our mindset.
If you’re an artist, producer, designer, or someone doing real work behind the scenes — and you’re looking for a space to grow, collaborate, or just be seen — hit us up.

Send us an email at umbralcollective@gmail.com with a short intro and a link to your work (portfolio, reel, Instagram — whatever shows what you do).
No egos. No gatekeeping. Just people who create.
